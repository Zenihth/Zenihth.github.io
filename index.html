About

    Since its inception, the idea to explore general applications of AI has been the source of inspiration of what we now term today as “defense safe artificial intelligence” (DSAI). Initially inspired by the advent of increasing cyber-crime, and use of modern technological advancements to conduct criminal activities, we began to explore tangible ways in which AI wasn’t only utility based but could also be a safety defense and counter crime mechanism.

    Our focus is not just to develop AI systems but to innovate them in ways that are pivotal to science and essential to global defense and safety strategies. The design and implementation of DSAI essentially seeks to create systems that intelligently counter crime, safeguard individuals, organizations and nations.

    “Through this pursuit, we hope to shape a future where AI is the cornerstone of safety, progress, and sustainable humanitarian betterment.”
